About Me

I am Khaukha Russel Peter, a dedicated theatre nurse with a passion for improving lives. I've had the honor of working in various fields, including theatre nursing, dental assistance, and organizing medical outreaches. One of the most memorable experiences in my career was during an outreach in Voi, where I treated over 600 patients, inspiring me to establish a hope center there.

Theatre Nurse

  • Patient Monitoring: Carefully monitored patient vitals during surgeries to ensure safety.

  • Post-Operative Care: Assisted in moving patients to recovery and monitored their progress post-surgery.
